Medical Research

Scientific studies continue to reveal the health benefits of regular fruit consumption.

Heart Health

Berries Reduce Cardiovascular Risk

Harvard Study, 2021 | 93,000 participants

Women who ate 3+ servings of blueberries and strawberries per week had a 34% lower risk of heart attack compared to those who ate less.

Read Study Summary
Brain Health

Citrus Fruits May Prevent Dementia

Tohoku University, 2022 | 13,000 participants

People who consumed citrus fruits daily had a 23% lower risk of developing dementia compared to those who ate them less than twice a week.

Read Study Summary
Diabetes

Whole Fruits Reduce Type 2 Diabetes Risk

BMJ, 2020 | 500,000 participants

Higher consumption of whole fruits, especially blueberries, grapes, and apples, was associated with a significantly lower risk of type 2 diabetes.

Read Study Summary
Cancer

Apples Show Anti-Cancer Properties

Italian Meta-Analysis, 2021 | 40 studies

Regular apple consumption was associated with reduced risk of lung cancer (23%), colorectal cancer (31%), and breast cancer (18%).

Read Study Summary
