Investment Opportunities

Discover ways to invest in the growing fruit agriculture sector, from direct farm investments to sustainable agriculture funds.

Direct Farm Ownership

Invest directly in fruit-producing farmland with our partner network. We connect investors with vetted farming operations in stable agricultural regions.

  • Average ROI: 8-12% annually
  • Minimum investment: $50,000
  • Typical holding period: 5-10 years

Sustainable Agriculture Funds

Diversified portfolios of fruit and nut farms managed by agricultural experts. Our partner funds focus on sustainable, climate-resilient practices.

  • Average ROI: 6-9% annually
  • Minimum investment: $10,000
  • Liquidity: Quarterly redemptions

Agricultural Technology

Invest in startups revolutionizing fruit farming through precision agriculture, robotics, and biological solutions.

  • High growth potential
  • Minimum investment: $5,000
  • Venture capital opportunities

Farmland REITs

Publicly traded Real Estate Investment Trusts that own and operate fruit-producing farmland. Provides liquidity and diversification.

  • Average dividend yield: 4-6%
  • No minimum investment
  • Daily liquidity
